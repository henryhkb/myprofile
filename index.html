About Me

I'm a software engineer and IT Manager based in Accra, Ghana, with hands-on experience building real-world systems in healthcare, transport, and education. My day job is managing the IT infrastructure of Korle Bu Teaching Hospital — one of Ghana's largest and most critical hospitals.
